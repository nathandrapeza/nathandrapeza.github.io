Poutine Tier List

Fri 08 October 2021
By Danger Doggo
Category: Blog

Poutine tier list for Edmonton:

1. La Poutine

    - high tier. I have much more to experience but this is really good.  
    - the only fancy/real poutine's I've ever tried

2. New York Fries

    - only real OG's know that the old menu contained: poutines, fries, and hot dogs... where did all these new poutines come from? also a large size? crazy!

3. Costco

    - you may be thinking... ????? but, in terms of size to price, costco is actually OP  
    - poutine isn't super amazing but is very big

4. McDonalds

    - I KNOW... very questionable 4th pick. but it's okay.

5. Harveys

    - okay it's starting to get not so good. I like harveys because theirs is kinda big  
    - does not taste that good lol

6. A&W

    - bad! stay away! money grab alert!  
    - tbh the mcdonalds one feels like a money grab too haha
